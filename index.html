Introduction

Rabbits are quite cute and fluffy, and we tend to chase them a lot. But how much do we know about them, and how are we supposed to take care of them well? Well, this website will show you all about the cute fluffball with ease! As you know, rabbits are seen a lot in town and in your neighborhood if in medium conditions, and kids are seen chasing them a lot! You can learn about the animal you're chasing right now!
